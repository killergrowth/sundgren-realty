* ONLINE ONLY AUCTION * * It will be your bid times 160 plus a 10% buyers premium added to the high bid to determine the contract price * PROPERTY DESCRIPTION: 160+- acres of pasture and trees with the North Fork of the Ninnescah River running through the property! What a unique opportunity to own a property with the diversity of waterfowl hunting on the river, Kansas Whitetail deer, turkeys, and much more!
