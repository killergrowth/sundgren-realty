About This Auction

PROPERTY DESCRIPTION: 40+- acres southeast of El Dorado, Kansas! This really nice property has everything you could want in a rural property; blacktop frontage, ponds, wet weather draws, trees, and beautiful native Flint Hills pasture! There is an old well with windmill remnants near the north central portion of the property. A brand new gate and entryway has recently been added along the blacktop Bluestem Rd. A rural water meter is set on the property and will transfer to the buyer! Great opportunity that you rarely see in rural Butler County, Kansas. The new buyer will receive cattle grazing income for the 2021 grass season. Beautiful future home building site Plus an outstanding opportunity to Invest In Land!
