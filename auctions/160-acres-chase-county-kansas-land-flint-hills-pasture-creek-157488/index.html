This exceptional quarter section sits in the heart of the scenic Flint Hills in Chase County—an area renowned for its rolling tallgrass prairie, productive native pasture, and unmatched natural beauty. This property offers a good stand of native grass ideal for cattle grazing, complemented by timber-lined draws that provide excellent wildlife habitat and natural protection for livestock. A live-water creek winds through the property, enhancing both its agricultural value and recreational appeal. Tucked into the center of the property is a charming small native limestone cabin—perfect as a weekend retreat, hunting basecamp, or quiet getaway to enjoy the peaceful surroundings and scenic views. With no water or electric, this cabin gives you a unique “old school” experience! Whether you’re looking to expand your cattle operation, invest in quality Flint Hills grassland, or secure a private recreational haven for hunting and outdoor enjoyment, this property delivers versatility and authentic Kansas character. Properties of this caliber in the Flint Hills are increasingly hard to find. Don’t miss the opportunity to Invest in the Flint Hills of Chase County, Kansas and Invest In Land!!
