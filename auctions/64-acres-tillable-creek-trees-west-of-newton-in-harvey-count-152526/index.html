PROPERTY DESCRIPTION: 64+- acres located west of Newton in Harvey County, Kansas with a nice variety of tillable farm ground, large mature trees and a long 1/2 mile plus stretch of Dry Creek running through the property. Access is provided by Emma Creek Rd along the east boundary, just 1/8 of a mile off blacktop. Quality and productive tillable farm ground makes up a large percentage of the property. The balance of the tract is prime hunting and recreation land. This area is known to have large Kansas whitetail deer, turkeys, quail and pheasants. Diverse smaller acreages with a combination of agriculture and hunting rarely become available in this area, making this a unique opportunity to Invest In Land! REAL ESTATE TAXES: This is part of a larger tax parcel LAND LOCATION FROM NEWTON: West on blacktop road NW 12th St 4 miles to Emma Creek Rd, south 1/8 mile to the property.
