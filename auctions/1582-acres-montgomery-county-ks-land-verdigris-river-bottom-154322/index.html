The solid home was built in 1951 with 1352 square feet of finished living area. In addition to the 2 car attached garage, there is an old 40′ x 40′ barn. Utilities to the house include rural water, electric, propane and septic. The terrain of the land is gently rolling towards the west end, then has a dramatic change in elevation at the east end with large tree covered hillsides. The largest of the two ponds sits just east of the house, while the second pond is tucked away in the timber covered hillside at the east end of the property. Both ponds offer enjoyment such as fishing and frequent waterfowl, as well as utility for livestock water. Regardless of your interest in livestock or hunting/wildlife/recreation, this property offers all at a premium! There are some BIG Kansas whitetail deer and a plethora of turkeys. A large portion of the property has been utilized as hay meadow, offering an additional diverse use or income. Access is provided by County Road 5800 along the north boundary, and County Road 3950 along the west. If you love high quality diverse land with a good solid house, take a look at this opportunity to Invest In Real Estate!
