About This Auction

* Property will sell by the acre, your bid times 326 to arrive at contract price * PROPERTY ADDRESS: 124 Ranch Rd., Grenola, KS 67346 PROPERTY DESCRIPTION: 3 bedroom 2 bath ranch style home on 326+- acres in a very scenic portion of the Kansas Flint Hills! The house was built in 1960 and has 1325 square feet on the main level plus a full basement. The main level has 2 bedrooms, 1 bathroom, kitchen, dining area, and a living room with fireplace. The basement has an additional bedroom, 1 bathroom, living area, laundry, AND a second kitchen! The setup would be perfect for ranch headquarters, hunting lodge, or weekend retreat. Buildings include a detached garage and multiple farm utility buildings. There is a nice set up livestock working pens. Traditional use of the pasture has been for cattle grazing. Seasonal summer grazing and year round cow/calf grazing are both suitable options. Over 1/2 mile of Union Creek runs from north to south through the property, offering live water in addition to the the 3 ponds. In addition to the quality livestock grazing opportunities, there is excellent hunting on the property especially along the western portion. High quality Kansas Whitetail Deer have been regularly viewed and harvested on the property. The elevation changes, high hills, plateau, and scenic view are impressive! Join us for the open house or schedule a private viewing of this property, and consider this opportunity to Invest In Land!
