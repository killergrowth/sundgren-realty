About This Auction

PROPERTY DESCRIPTION: 74+- acres of tillable farm ground between Bentley and Maize, northwest of Wichita in Sedgwick County, Kansas. This property consists of all class II soils, offering an excellent opportunity to invest in agriculture. There are multiple rural residential developments in the area, providing some potential development value in the future. Access is provide by township roads on the south and west boundaries. Approximately 25 acres of the property is currently planted to wheat. The Buyer will receive cash rent following the harvest of that wheat crop. Possession of the property will be at closing subject to that wheat harvest. EARNEST MONEY: $40,000 2022 REAL ESTATE TAXES: $852.41 LAND LOCATION: From Bentley, Kansas 1/2 mile east on 109th St North to 135th St West, then 3/4 mile south to the property. TERMS: Bidding will be by the acre, your bid times 74. There will be a 10% buyers premium added to the high bid to determine the contract price. The successful high bidder shall be required to sign the real estate purchase contract linked above and be purchasing the property subject to the preliminary title search report also linked above. Non refundable earnest money is stated above and due at the conclusion of the auction unless other arrangements have been made by Buyer prior to bidding. The balance shall be due upon Sellers submission of merchantable title and closing. Closing date shall be on or before January 20, 2023. Closing fee and title Insurance fee will be split 50/50 between Seller and Buyer. Selling subject to easements, restrictions, roadways and rights of way. This property is not selling subject to financing, inspections, or appraisal. Interested buyers need to view the property prior to the date of the auction and have financing available and any inspections performed prior to bidding. Boundary lines are not guaranteed. All pertinent information is available upon request.
