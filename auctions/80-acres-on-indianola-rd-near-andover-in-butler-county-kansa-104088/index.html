About This Auction

* ONLINE ONLY AUCTION *   Anna Hancock Ralstin Revocable Trust dated March 27, 2009, Seller PROPERTY DESCRIPTION: 80+- acres located southeast of Andover in Butler County, Kansas. Tremendous opportunity to own land in a rural location, yet just 2 1/2 miles from Flint Hills National Golf Course! There is a rural water meter and electric meter on the property, offering utilities to build a house, barn home, or cabin. A nice pond is nestled into a large timber lined draw that runs east and west through a large majority of the property. In addition to the pond and trees, a large portion of the property is native pasture offering the opportunity to graze horses or livestock. Most of the property is not visible from the road frontage due to a large hill, offering the opportunity to build in a private non-visible location! An old shed could offer some utility or vintage materials to repurpose. The old saying goes, "Location, Location, Location"...and this property certainly has that! Invest In Land! LEGAL DESCRIPTION: The North Half of the Southeast Quarter of Section 10, Township 28 South, Range 3 East of the 6th P.M., Butler County, Kansas.
