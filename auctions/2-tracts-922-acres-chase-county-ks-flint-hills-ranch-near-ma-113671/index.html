EACH TRACT WILL SELL BY THE ACRE, YOUR BID TIMES THE ACREAGE TO DETERMINE CONTRACT PRICE TRACT 1 PROPERTY DESCRIPTION: 604+- acres located between Matfield Green and Wonsevu in the Flint Hills of Chase County, Kansas.
