This land is primarily established in native Bluestem grasses, making it ideal for cattle grazing or livestock operations. A pond provides a water source for livestock and wildlife. Trees are scattered throughout the property, offering natural shelter for livestock and creating habitat for wildlife. The mix of open pasture and tree cover makes this an attractive setting for livestock, hunting/recreation, enjoying the outdoors, or a future home building site. In addition to its agricultural and recreational features, the property includes oil production income, adding an investment component and long-term value. Mineral rights in the area tend to be consistent and longstanding. Access is excellent, with township gravel road all along the east boundary. HWY 254 is just over 1/2 mile north, and blacktop SW 20th St is just 1/4 mile south. Whether you’re looking to expand a cattle operation, invest in land with income, or build a rural home with privacy and convenience, this tract checks all the boxes.
