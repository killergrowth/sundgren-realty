Water features include the a 20 acre Watershed Lake, 5 ponds, a creek running into the Watershed Lake, a portion of an upper branch of Nicholas Creek, and the spillway pool and creek exiting the Watershed Lake! That’s a lot of water features PLUS a rural water meter transfers with the property to the buyer! Most of the balance of the property is split between native Flint Hills bluestem pasture and trees. Excellent access is provided by blacktop Flinthills Rd along the west boundary, and township gravel road along portions of the interior, southern, and eastern boundaries. There’s just no end to desirable characteristics to this property! Take a look at this incredible opportunity to Invest In Land!
