About This Auction

Harris Chandler, Seller TRACT 1 PROPERTY ADDRESS: 11498 SW Boyer Rd., Augusta, Ks 67010 PROPERTY DESCRIPTION:  40+- acres southeast of Augusta in Butler County, Kansas with an 84′ x 50′ building. With good township gravel road access along the west boundary, this property sits just 1 1/2 miles from blacktop 100th and 1 1/4 miles from blacktop Haverhill Rd. Water is provided by a good well, with lines run to hydrants and automatic waterers. Electric is buried from the road and services both the barn and pens east of the barn. With a large amount of quality pipe pens, traps and concrete bunks, this property is set up excellent for your livestock operation. Inside the barn there is a tack room for storage. Most of the building has concrete floors, but there are some dirt floor areas for livestock. Water is plumbed for a wash rack at the southwest corner of the building. Native Flint Hills pasture makes up the balance of the property. With the gently rolling terrain, you can utilize the pasture for hay or grazing. With all of these great amenities already in place, this would be the perfect place to build your dream home in the country! LEGAL DESCRIPTION: N/2 W/2 SW/4 Section 33, Township 27 South, Range 5 East of the 6th PM, Butler County, Kansas TRACT 2 PROPERTY ADDRESS: 2918 SW 120TH, Augusta, Ks 67010 PROPERTY DESCRIPTION: 40+- acres of native Flint Hills pasture with township gravel road access along two sides of the property, located at the northeast corner of Boyer Rd and 120th. This tract is just 3/4 mile from blacktop Haverhill Rd and 1 3/4 mile from blacktop 100th. Butler Rural Water District #4 has a 4″ waterline at the southwest corner of Boyer and 120th. With both water and electricity available, this tract is an excellent candidate for a rural residence!
