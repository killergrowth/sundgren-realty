This 2 bedroom 2 bath home has a full unfinished basement, allowing additional bedrooms or more living space as you see fit! Built in 1983, this ranch style home has 1540 square feet of finished living area on the main floor. That space includes both bedrooms and bathrooms, kitchen with breakfast eating area, a formal dining space, living room, and large laundry room. Each bedroom has a nice big walk-in closet! What a great opportunity to live in rural Butler County, Kansas with excellent blacktop access!
