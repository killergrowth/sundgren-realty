About This Auction

TRACT 1: 726.7 acres with Middle Caney Creek, multiple ponds, pasture, and timber with a homestead located in the Hills of Chautauqua County, Kansas northwest of Sedan. TRACT 2: 140.8 acres with Sycamore Creek, pasture, pond, and timber with blacktop frontage along HWY 166 between Sedan and Cedar Vale in Chautauqua County, Kansas. TRACT 3: 155.8 acres of pasture and a large tree and brush covered hillside intersected by an abandoned railroad track between Sedan and Cedar Vale in Chautauqua County, Kansas just 5 miles north of the Oklahoma border.
