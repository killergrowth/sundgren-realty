About This Auction

ADDRESS: 7646 NW 60TH ST, EL DORADO KS 67042. HOUSE: Premier 7 bedroom 6 bath ranch style home with upgrades galore! As you walk through the custom iron front door into the entryway, you are immediately met with a massive living room with a wood burning fireplace, high vaulted ceilings, and wood accent beams. Custom floor to ceiling windows overlook the pool, pool house, garden, and chicken mansion. The bar area is nestled next to the glass and has heated tile floors with interior grill and vent hood. Truly a beautiful place to entertain or have an evening cocktail. The large open kitchen/dining area has every upgrade you could imagine for including multiple sinks, propane cooktop, dual ovens, and a separate main floor galley kitchen for behind the scenes prep when entertaining. Other main level features include multiple pantries, high end wood flooring, quality countertops, and upgraded light fixtures. Both the main level and basement have a laundry room. The basement has a huge recreation room with heated tile and walk out doors to the pool area. A custom wine cellar complete with LED backlit accent wall, humidity and temperature control, and glass front with security lock provides the perfect place for your wine collection. Enjoy summer days in the custom saltwater swimming pool/spa with in floor cleaning system, deck jets/water fountains, and propane fire pit. Custom stonework and IPE deck complement and attest to the quality of work throughout the property. The pool house has heated floors, air conditioning, dry sauna, full entertaining kitchen, ice maker, full bathroom, and a bedroom that sleeps four. LAND: 220+- acres with a combination of native Flint Hills pasture, Alfalfa, and Timber.  Fourmile Creek and a smaller creek meet on the property, providing two live water sources to attract and hold wildlife. The driveway is covered with asphalt millings for clean all weather entry. An abundance of wildlife includes quail, turkey, and world class deer. Groomed riding paths throughout the property offer scenic riding for the avid equestrian. Native limestone rocks sourced on the property have been used extensively in landscaping. There are two water wells for livestock and irrigation, with the house being serviced by rural water. The garden has full irrigation controls (automatic drip) with a massive custom fountain and colored concrete pathways. Truly a dream home and property in every way! BUILDINGS: Several quality buildings include a 60′ x 36′ wood barn ideal for equipment with built in tool storage, concrete floor, and a hay loft. The 60′ x 45′ horse barn has a riding pen and three rail wood accent fence. A 70′ x 36′ hay & equipment barn and other misc. storage buildings provide you with an abundance of storage. Regardless of your intent for recreation vehicle storage or livestock use, this property is set up and ready!
