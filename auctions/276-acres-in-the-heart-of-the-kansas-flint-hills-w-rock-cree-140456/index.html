An exceptionally good stretch of Rock Creek runs through the center of the property, with nearly 1/4 mile of native pasture and limestone filtered water full of life! From fish such as largemouth bass, bluegill, and channel catfish to fun water animals such as frogs, crawdads and tadpoles…this pristine water source is a feature difficult to find without buying a large ranch. Perfectly situated off blacktop just west of the Latham in Butler County, KS.
