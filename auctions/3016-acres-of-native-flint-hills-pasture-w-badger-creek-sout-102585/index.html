About This Auction

PROPERTY DESCRIPTION: 301.6 acres located southeast of Winfield in Cowley County, Kansas. What a great property! Badger Creek runs through the southeast corner of the property. This tremendous feature provides live water for livestock and recreation opportunities such as fishing and hunting. A large majority of the property is gently rolling native Flint Hills pasture. There is a pond located in the west central portion of the property. Multiple wet weather draws provide additional water sources throughout the property. Township roads border the perimeter of the property on the north and west, plus on a portion of the south and east. The very southeast corner of the property has formerly been utilized as tillable farm ground, and is not included in the perimeter fence that encompasses the property. Take advantage of this opportunity to purchase a high quality cattle or livestock grazing pasture with bonus recreation enjoyment opportunities! Invest In Land!
