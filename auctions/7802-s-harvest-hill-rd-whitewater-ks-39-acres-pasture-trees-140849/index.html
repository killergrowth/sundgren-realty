PROPERTY ADDRESS: 7802 S Harvest Hill Rd., Whitewater, KS 67154 PROPERTY DESCRIPTION: 39 acres southwest of Whitewater in Harvey County, Kansas. This diverse property has as combination of bluestem grasses, trees, and a 50′ x 30′ building built in 1998. There is an active water well and active electric on the property. Harvey County Rural Water District #1 has a 1 1/2″ line along the west side of Harvest Hill Rd. The pasture was once enrolled in the CRP program but that has expired, allowing use of that grass how you see fit. Most recently, it has been cut for prairie hay. Wildlife is active and abundant on the property including Kansas whitetail deer, quail, turkey and an occasional pheasant. Take a look at this opportunity to own land within a reasonable proximity to Whitewater, Newton and Wichita! Invest In Land!
