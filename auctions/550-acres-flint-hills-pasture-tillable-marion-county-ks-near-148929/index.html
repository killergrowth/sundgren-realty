Auction Date: TUESDAY OCTOBER 28, 2025 6:00 PM Auction Location: EL DORADO CIVIC CENTER, 201 E CENTRAL AVE, EL DORADO KS Randy W. Smith and Elizabeth K. Smith Joint Revocable Trust dated December 15, 2022, Seller PROPERTY DESCRIPTION: 550 acres located southwest of Florence in Marion County, Kansas. This quality tract of land consists of approximately 495 acres of native Flint Hills pasture and 55+- acres of tillable and trees in the northeast corner. The tillable is currently planted to soybeans. Water is provided by natural springs, wet weather draws, and water wells. Additional income is provided by active oil production on the property, of which the landowner receives a 1/8 royalty interest. Access is good, just 2 miles of township gravel road off blacktop HWY 77. Finding such a quality, sizable and diverse property is difficult, take a look at this excellent opportunity to Invest In Land!
