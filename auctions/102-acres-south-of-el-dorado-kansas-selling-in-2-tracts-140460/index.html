AUCTIONEERS NOTE: Two great land buying options south of El Dorado, Kansas for investment, agriculture or future home building sites! TRACT 1: 46.3 acres TRACT 2: 55.7 acres LAND HIGHLIGHTS: Excellent opportunity to purchase land south of El Dorado in Butler County, Kansas.
