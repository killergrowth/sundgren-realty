About This Auction

* ONLINE ONLY AUCTION * PROPERTY DESCRIPTION: 148 acres located southwest of Scott City in Scott County, Kansas. This property is 100% tillable, with all Class II and Class III soils. Approximately 57% of the property is Richfield Silt Loam soil, 0 to 1 percent slope, and the balance of the property is Ulysses Silt Loam and mostly all also 0 to 1 percent slope. If you are looking to expand your existing farm operation or expand into agriculture, take a look at this opportunity to Invest In Land! LEGAL DESCRIPTION: The Southwest Quarter of Section 18, Township 19 South, Range 34 West of the 6th P.M., Scott County, Kansas. Subject to public road. LAND LOCATION: From Scott City, 11 miles west on HWY 96 to Wichita Scott Rd, 5 1/2 miles south to the property. 2023 REAL ESTATE TAXES: $1,153.98 TERMS: Bidding will close at 4:00 PM Central on Thursday January 18, 2024 or once no additional bids have been placed for 2 minutes.
