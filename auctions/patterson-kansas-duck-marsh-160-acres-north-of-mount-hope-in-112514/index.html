About This Auction

* ONLINE ONLY LAND AUCTION * PROPERTY DESCRIPTION: 160+- acres located north of Mount Hope in Harvey County, Kansas featuring 8+- acres of irrigated marsh. In addition to migratory birds such as ducks, geese and turtle doves calling this property home on a seasonal basis, there is a good population of upland game birds such as pheasants and quail PLUS Kansas whitetail deer on the property year-round. If you are a waterfowl enthusiast, this is a unique opportunity to purchase an established irrigated marsh habitat in the North American Central Flyway and Kansas Low Plains Late Zone. Smart weed and other waterfowl favored vegetation and roost cover is naturally occurring throughout the marsh and surrounding low lying area. If you are looking for turnkey, this marsh is ready to hunt with (3) 2-occupant pit blinds, (1) 3-occupant pit blind, and an additional shallow pit to reduce the height of an above ground blind. Plenty of cover plants, shrubs and grasses surround the marsh to aid in “bruising in” your blinds and/or provide concealment for above ground hunting. An irrigation well is located on the property with a 6″ waterline to the marsh for bottom filling. Water rights transfer with the property. The grade of the marsh varies to allow for numerous feeding depths. Depths of over 1′ can be obtained while still allowing areas in the 6″ to 8″ range for a mixed variety of birds.  An old abandoned railroad track angles through the southwest corner of the property, offering habitat and a common traffic way for wildlife. While the irrigated marsh is the main water feature on the property, there is also a natural marsh marsh in the southwest corner of the property and several other areas that water is common and could offer additional waterfowl habitat development. Like new steel post and 5 strand barbed wire is in place offering the opportunity for summer livestock grazing. This truly is a unique opportunity to purchase such a diverse property in Central Kansas…take advantage of this opportunity to Invest In Kansas Land! WETLAND EASEMENT: A portion of this property is under a permanent wetlands easement with the Unites States of America to “restore, protect, manage, maintain, and enhance the functional values of wetlands and other lands, and for the conservation of natural values including fish and wildlife and their habitat, water quality improvement, flood water retention, groundwater recharge, open space, aesthetic values, and environmental education.” See full documentation below.
