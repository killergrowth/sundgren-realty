PROPERTY DESCRIPTION: 128.8 acres of Cowley County, Kansas land between Latham and Cambridge. Grouse Creek enters the property at two different locations, offering excellent fishing and creek activity enjoyment opportunities. A majority of the property is a brome/fescue mixture that the current owner has utilized for hay, making 360 bales in 2025 weighing 1400 lbs each. The grass is cross fenced, with the north unit being utilized in the past for grazing. Soils types are nearly all good quality Class I and Class II Ivan silt loam and Brewer silty clay loam, making this property an excellent candidate for tillable farm ground use as well!
