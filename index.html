Who We Are

For over 40 years, Sundgren Realty has exemplified a steadfast commitment to service, creativity, honesty and hard work. Our vast skillset is perfectly suited to guide our clients as they engage in buying and selling residential property, farm/ranch/recreational acreage, and commercial property in South Central Kansas.

We pride ourselves on our knowledge of Butler County and the surrounding counties and feel most fortunate to be located in the middle of the majestic Flint Hills – a region unlike any other!

While the real estate market has grown in value and complexity over the last several decades, one thing remains the same: our commitment to understanding your individual needs and providing exceptional real estate experiences. Our greatest reward is to help you realize your dream of ownership.
